The Beginning

Yada Pruksachatkun has always been intrigued with the power of dance and empowerment, having been transformed by it herself. A native of Chiang Mai, Thailand, and a graduate at THINK Global School, the first traveling high school in the world, she helped hospital youth starting with the best way she knows how: through dance. Now, she hopes to empower hospitalized youth to find and pursue their passion, which she had found in dance. She is currently a college student at Wellelsey College, and has handed over her presidency as of September 2015, after two yaars of overseeing the growth of the organization.
